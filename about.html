About Us

Born and raised in the city of Angels, Ty Lee, the owner of H-Thai-ML, had a passion for cuisine. With her background culture and desire to whip up meals, Lee was sure she can turn her creative hobby into an investment of expanding her cuisine statewide.

Los Angeles was the start of it all and Lee was ambitious to further grow her passion into a full time career.Since LA, the business has expanded to three other major locations.
